Recipe for the perfect meme

    Ingredients:

  • 500ml of image (dank if possible)
  • 69g of epic text describing what is happening in your meme

    Procedure:

  1. Go to any meme generator website, in this case https://imgflip.com/memegenerator
  2. Upload your desired image file
  3. Enter your text in the "Text 1, 2, etc." boxes, and move the boxes on the images to your liking
  4. Click "Generate Meme" and enjoy!
